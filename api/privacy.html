Privacy Policy for Unpack

Effective Date: December 15, 2024

1. Overview

Unpack ("we", "our", or "us") is a Chrome Extension dedicated to simplifying text content on the web. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal identification information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. The extension only processes data explicitly selected by you.

  • User Content: When you use the "Unpack this" feature, the specific text you selected is sent to our server to be processed by an AI model.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous metrics (e.g., number of requests) to ensure service stability.

3. Data Usage

The text you select is used solely for the purpose of generating a simplified summary. This data is:

  • Sent securely (via HTTPS) to our processing server.
  • Forwarded to a third-party AI provider (e.g., Anthropic or Google) for simplification.
  • NOT permanently stored on our servers.
  • NOT sold to third parties or used for advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party LLM providers to perform the text simplification. Their privacy practices are governed by their respective policies.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support page.
